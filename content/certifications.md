## CompTIA Security+ (SY0-701)
**Issued by:** CompTIA  
**Earned:** 2025  

The CompTIA Security+ certification validates foundational knowledge in cybersecurity principles and best practices. It demonstrates proficiency in identifying threats, securing systems and networks, and applying risk-based security controls in real-world environments.

**Key areas covered:**
- Security Operations and Incident Response  
- Network Security and Architecture  
- Threats, Vulnerabilities, and Mitigations  
- Identity and Access Management (IAM)  
- Risk Management and Governance  

This certification aligns with entry-level security roles such as **SOC Analyst**, **Security Analyst**, and **Cybersecurity Intern**.
